Recipe #1

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
pasta of your choice 200g

Recipe #2

  • Time: 50 minutes
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients
400g caster sugar 225g plain flour 65g best quality cocoa powder 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder 1 1/2 teaspoons bicarbonate of soda 1 teaspoon salt 2 eggs 250ml milk 125ml vegetable oil 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 250ml boiling water

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas 4. Grease and flour two 23cm round cake tins.

Step 2: In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, bicarb and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Cake mixture will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared tins.

Step 3: Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a skewer. Cool in the tins for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
